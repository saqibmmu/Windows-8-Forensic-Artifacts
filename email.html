Email for Students and Staff

Using Microsoft Office 365 A reliable and secure email services accessible by all students. We have partnered with Microsoft and Goggle Mail to provide all of our students with enterprise-grade email in the cloud, offering 50GB mailboxes, Office integration and tools to help facilitate collaboration. These services is available 24 hours a day, from any device, anywhere in the world You can access student email via the University or, failing that, you can go via Microsoft's mail.office365.com or any other mail services login page, using your usual University email address and password.
