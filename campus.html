About Arthur John Building

The Arthur John Building is situated on the Campus on Walter Road and it is suitable for many types of events particularly large conferences. It is extremely well located and has one reception area on the Walter Road. It is a 5 minute walk from the Walter Street Train station. The Arthur John Building is a large impressive modern building. It is famous for "The Street", a large, ground floor, contemporary walkway which links the two parts of the building. "The Street" is a perfect open plan space for exhibitions, networking receptions and hospitality. This spacious area connects the main lecture theatres, meeting rooms and hospitality areas on the ground floor. The main Assembly Hall lecture theatre has retractable seating and offers another excellent, flexible space.(164 square metres). Further lecture theatres are available on the upper floors.
